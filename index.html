How to Stake

Daedalus

Daedalus is a secure desktop only wallet in which you can hold your ADA currency.

Installation

  1. Download the Daedalus wallet from their website
  2. Install and open the wallet onto your machine.

    3. Note: When opening Daedalus for the first time, it is normal for it to take a while to sync. This process will be much faster the next time you open it.

Create and fund your wallet

  1. Click "Add wallet" at the bottom left of the window.
    - Create: Creates a wallet with new recovery phrase.
    - Pair: Pairs your Daedalus with your hardware wallet (Ledger or Trezor).
    - Restore: Allows you to import an existing wallet from its recovery phrase.
    - Import: Import from earlier version of Daedalus or from the Daedalus state directory.
  2. If you create a new wallet, make sure you back up your 24 word recovery phrase in PHYSICAL form.
    We recommend the Cobo Tablet Plus.
  3. Once the wallet is created, and you backed up your recovery phrase, click on the "Receive" tab.
  4. Click one of the "Receiving Addresses" and click "Copy address".
    All of these addresses are connected to the same wallet and its recovery phrase. Once your use an address to receive funds, it gets greyed out. You should only use an address ONCE to protect your privacy (learn more).
  5. Go to your Crypto Exchange and send funds to this address.
    If you are planning to send a significant amount of ADA, it's always safest to send a test transaction to make sure the address was copied over correctly.

Stake your ADA

When staking on Cardano, you don't actually send your ADA anywhere! Instead, it's a trustless process in which you simply assign your wallet to a stake pool. That is how the protocol knows how much to pay out to you in rewards. This also means, any time you add more ADA to your delegated wallet, it automatically gets staked and earns rewards for you.
Note: When you first stake ADA, there is a waiting period of 3 full epochs (or 15-20 days) before you start earning rewards.

  1. Go to the "Delegation Center" (2nd option in the left sidebar).
  2. Click on the "Stake pools" tab.
  3. Enter "SPRO" into the search bar to find our pool, select it and then click "Delegate to this pool".

That's it. Your wallet is now delegated to a stake pool and will earn you rewards after the 3 epoch waiting period is over. You can continue receiving and spending from you wallet as you would with any other wallet.
