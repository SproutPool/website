Staking - Daedalus

Overview

On this page you will learn how to install Daedalus, a secure, desktop only, wallet in which you can hold your ADA currency. You will also learn how to create and fund your wallet, as well as delegate it to [SPRO] so you can start earning those sweet rewards.

Step 1: Installation

  1. Download the Daedalus wallet from their official website
  2. Install and open the wallet on your machine.

    3. Note: When opening Daedalus for the first time, it is normal for it to take a while to sync. This process will be much faster the next time you open it.

Step 2: Create your wallet

- Create: Creates a fresh wallet with a new recovery phrase.
- Pair: Pairs Daedalus with your hardware wallet (Ledger/Trezor).
- Restore: Allows you to import an existing wallet using its recovery phrase.
- Import: Import from earlier version of Daedalus or from the Daedalus state directory.

IMPORTANT:
Save your recovery phrase ONLY in physical form! Do NOT store it on your computer, phone or any other electronic device.
We recommend storing your seed phrase using a Cobo Tablet Plus.

Wait until your wallet is synced to 100%

Step 3: Fund your wallet

All of these addresses are connected to the same wallet and its recovery phrase. Once you use an address to receive funds, it gets greyed out. You should only use an address ONCE to protect your privacy.

If you haven't already, purchase some ADA at an exchange of your choice. We recommend:
-- Kraken ( 0.6 ADA withdrawal fee )
-- Crypto.com ( 2 ADA withdrawal fee )
Once finished, send funds to the address copied above.

If you are planning to send a significant amount of ADA, it's always safest to send a test transaction using a smaller amount (~20 ADA) to make sure the address was copied over correctly.

Step 4: Stake your ADA

Make sure to type "SPRO" into the search bar to find our pool ;)

Once you finished the remaining steps, your wallet will be delegated to our pool!

A few things to keep in mind:
-- All of your funds stay in your wallet. We have no control over them.
-- Any funds added to the delegated wallet will automatically be staked (including rewards); you don't have to do anything.
-- You will start receiving rewards 15-20 days after your initial delegation. Rewards will then be received at the end of every epoch (5 days).
-- A pool is likely to mint 1 block per epoch once it reaches an active stake above ~1.2M ADA.
